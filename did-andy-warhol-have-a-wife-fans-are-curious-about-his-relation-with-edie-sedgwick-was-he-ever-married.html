Andy Warhol’s Relation With Edie Sedgwick Explored

Andy Warhol and Edie Sedgwick were considered one of the greatest romances of 1960. They were called the pop art’s golden couple.

In fact, it was Warhol who is responsible for making Edie Sedgwick a superstar in the world of fashion. She had acted in 10 movies of Warhol.

They had first met on March 26, 1965, at a birthday party for an American playwright, Tennessee Williams.

Their final official movie was titled, Lupe, released more than 50 years ago.

According to Vanityfair, the two were completely opposite of one another. It was possibly the source of their attraction.

However, their obsession for one another had only lasted for a year.
