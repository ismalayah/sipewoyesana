Ben Roethlisberger popularly known as Benjamin Todd Roethlisberger Sr. is an American football quarterback. As of 2022, Ben Roethlisberger’s net worth is $100 million. He is nicknamed ‘Big Ben’. He attended Miami University and was taken in the first round (11th overall) in the 2004 NFL Draft by the Steelers.

He won the Associated Press NFL Offensive Rookie of the Year Award in 2004 and was named to his first Pro Bowl in 2007. He became the NFL’s youngest Super Bowl champion in 2006, guiding the Steelers to a 21–10 victory against the Seattle Seahawks in Super Bowl XL at the age of 23.
