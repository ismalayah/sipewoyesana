Willa Fitzgerald (born January 17, 1991) is an American actress born in Nashville, Tennessee. She is known for recurring roles on Alpha House and Royal Pains and for the lead role as Emma Duval in MTV/Netflix’s Scream. Fitzgerald earned a BA in theater studies from Yale University in 2013.
