Kaitlyn Dever Biography

Kaitlyn Dever is among the youngest upcoming American actresses. Her first role was in the American Girl: Chrissa Stands Strong as Gwen Thompson. She has also appeared in Make It Break It as Adorable Girl and Modern family as Bianca Douglas. She has also starred in the crime scene ‘Justified’

Justified really boosted her dream career. She has always been interested in acting since when she was 5. Currently, she has worked in more than 15 different movies and she is working on another one that will soon be released. Her most recent movie was released in 2019 called Booksmart.

Down to her minor roles, she has appeared in television series like Modern Family, The Mentalist, and Party down, among others. She has also appeared in several music videos and has performed voiceover for two different video games.

Kaitlyn has been nominated severally for several awards and has won four among them. She has won Newport Beach Film Festival Awards, Ensemble Cast, CinemaCon Awards, Female stars of tomorrow, and Hollywood Critics Association Awards.

How old is Kaitlyn Dever? Dever is 25 years old as of 2021. She was born on December 21, 1996, in Phoenix, Arizona, the United States. Therefore, she celebrates her birthday on December 21st every year and her birth sign is Sagittarius.

Kaitlyn Dever Family

Dever was born in Phoenix, Arizona, and did most of her growing up in Dallas, Texas. Her parents are Kathy Dever and Tim Dever who were both ice skating coaches.
