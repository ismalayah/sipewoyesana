Robert Plant Career

Plant began his musical journey in 1968 when he was chosen by musician Jimmy Page as the lead singer of his band.  The band was later changed to Led Zeppelin and they released their first album on January 12, 1969 and it recorded commercial success. On October 22, 1969, the band released their second album titled ‘Led Zeppelin II’, their second album was a massive success and the album topped music charts in the United States and the United Kingdom.

On October 5, 1970, they released their album titled ‘Led Zeppelin III’ and they went on to release more albums like ‘Houses of the Holy’, ‘Physical Graffiti’, ‘In Through the Door’ and more. The Led Zeppelin band split in 1980 after the death of Bonham on September 25, 1980. This prompted Plant to go solo.
