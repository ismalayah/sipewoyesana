Biography

Ilham (born in 1997), whose real name is Ilham Karaoui, is a gifted Moroccan-American singer and songwriter. With her mellifluous vocals, enchanting melodies, and a touch of nostalgia, she has captured the hearts of critics and fans alike. 

Renowned publications such as Flaunt Magazine, Vogue Arabia, and AZEEMA have celebrated her musical prowess, recognizing her as a star in her craft.

In 2022, Ilham unveiled her debut EP, titled 41-10, a sonic marvel that garnered widespread acclaim, introducing her to a broader audience. Building on this success, she followed up with her second EP, With Time, in 2023. This release surpassed even the lofty expectations set by her debut, establishing Ilham as one of the most exciting emerging artists of our time.

Career

Ilham, a music star with Moroccan roots, discovered her passion for crafting lyrical journeys from an early age.
